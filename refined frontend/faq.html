Symptom Checker FAQ

  • 1. What is this Symptom Checker?
    Our Symptom Checker is an online tool that helps you get a preliminary understanding of potential health conditions based on your symptoms. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice.
  • 2. How does it work?
    You input your symptoms into the tool, and it matches them against a database of common diseases. It then provides a ranked list of possible conditions for informational purposes.
  • 3. Is it accurate?
    While the system uses a curated database of symptoms and diseases, it cannot guarantee a diagnosis. For accurate medical advice, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.
  • 4. Do I need to create an account to use it?
    Basic symptom checking can be done without an account, but creating an account allows you to save past checks, track symptoms, and access personalized recommendations.
  • 5. Can children or elderly use this tool?
    Yes, but symptom interpretation may vary by age. Always consult a doctor for children, the elderly, or patients with chronic conditions.
  • 6. Are my health data and symptoms private?
    Yes, all data is stored securely. We do not share personal health data with third parties. Always read our Privacy Policy for details.
  • 7. What should I do if the symptom checker suggests a serious condition?
    If a serious condition is indicated, seek medical attention immediately. Do not rely solely on the tool for urgent symptoms.
  • 8. Can I use it to replace my doctor visits?
    No. This tool is meant for educational and informational purposes only. Always consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
  • 9. Why does it show multiple possible conditions?
    Symptoms often overlap across diseases. The tool shows all potential matches ranked by likelihood, so you can discuss them with your doctor.
  • 10. Can I suggest new symptoms or conditions?
    If your experience suggests missing data, you can submit suggestions via our feedback form to help improve the tool.
